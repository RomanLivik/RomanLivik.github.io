Background

Who I am

I'm a student at Moscow Technical University of Communications and Informatics, diving deep into the world of IT every single day — not just as a career path, but as a genuine passion and lifestyle.

My interests span unix-based systems, application development, graphical design, OS customization, ethical hacking and security research. More than half of my projects I build entirely solo, from concept to deployment.

GNU/Linux is my daily driver and my favourite platform. I love ricing, writing automation scripts, building themes, and contributing tools that make life easier for the broader Linux community.
